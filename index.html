About me

I grew up on a family farm, surrounded by nature and hard work. As a child, I actively participated in our farm's operations, helping out at farmers' markets and engaging with customers. These experiences cultivated my strong communication skills and taught me the value of interpersonal connections.

Driven by a desire to serve and make a difference, I pursued a career in firefighting. I dedicated myself to the rigorous training required to become a firefighter and obtained certification as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT). This allowed me to provide vital medical assistance to those in need, further deepening my commitment to helping others during challenging times.

Seeking new opportunities, I transitioned into the field of electronics assembly. With a keen interest in precision and craftsmanship, I specialized in magnetics and gained expertise in building transformers for the defense industry. This role demanded meticulous attention to detail and a thorough understanding of complex electrical systems. It provided me with a unique perspective on the intersection of technology and national security.

Throughout my diverse professional journey, I have developed a strong work ethic, adaptability, and a passion for continuous learning. I believe that my diverse background and varied experiences have equipped me with a well-rounded skill set, allowing me to approach challenges with a unique perspective and deliver exceptional results. I am excited to leverage my skills and experiences in new endeavors, contributing to meaningful projects and making a positive impact in the world.
